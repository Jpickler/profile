Information on Dachshunds

General Information

The dachshund, also known as the wiener dog, badger dog, doxie, and sausage dog, is a short-legged, long-bodied, hound-type dog breed. The dog may be smooth-haired, wire-haired, or long-haired. Coloration varies. The dachshund was bred to scent, chase, and flush out badgers and other burrow-dwelling animals.

Life expectancy

12 to 16 years

Colors

Black, Black & Tan, Chocolate & Tan, Blue & Tan, Chocolate & Cream, Cream, Tan, Red, Piebald

Origin

Germany

Scientific Name

Canis lupus familiaris

Temperament

Lively, Clever, Playful, Devoted, Stubborn, Active, Independent, Courageous
